\begin{exercise}
Consider the planes:
\[
2x-y-z=1\quad\text{and}\quad x+2y-z=2
\]
Find a vector valued function that runs along their intersection.
\[
\vecl(t) = \vector{\answer{2+3t},1+t,\answer{2+5t}}
\]
\end{exercise}

\begin{exercise}
Consider the planes:
\[
x+y+z=0\quad\text{and}\quad-x+y-z=4
\]
Find a vector valued function that runs along their intersection.
\[
\vecl(t) = \vector{-1-2t,\answer{2},\answer{-1+2t}}
\]
\end{exercise}

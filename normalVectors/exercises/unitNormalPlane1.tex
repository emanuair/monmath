\begin{exercise}
Consider the plane:
\[
3x-2y+z=7
\]
Find a unit vector normal to the plane with positive $z$-component.
\[
\unormal = \vector{\answer{3/\sqrt{14}},\answer{-\sqrt{2/7}},\answer{1/\sqrt{14}}}
\]
\end{exercise}

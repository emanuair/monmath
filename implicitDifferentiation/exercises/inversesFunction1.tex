\begin{exercise}

Based on the following table of values, calculate the indicated quantities:

 \[
  \begin{array}{|c|c|c|}\hline
    x & f  & f' \\ \hline \hline
    1 & 2 & -1 \\ \hline
    2 & 0  & 2  \\ \hline
    3 & 1  & -2 \\ \hline
    4 & -3 & 0  \\ \hline
  \end{array}
  \]

The point $(1, \answer{3})$ is on the graph of $f^{-1}$.

\[
(f^{-1})'(1) = \answer{-\frac{1}{2}}
\]

\end{exercise}

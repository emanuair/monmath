\begin{exercise}

Consider the relation $x = 2$.  Implicitly differentiating with
respect to $x$, we seem to obtain $1 = 0$.
\begin{multipleChoice}
  \choice{This is a valid use of implicit differentiation}
	\choice[correct]{This is not a valid use of implicit differentiation}
\end{multipleChoice}
Explain why or why not.  Compare and contrast with the case of  $y=2$.
\end{exercise}

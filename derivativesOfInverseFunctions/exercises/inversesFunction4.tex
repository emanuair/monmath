\begin{exercise}

Based on the following table of values, calculate the indicated quantities:

 \[
  \begin{array}{|c|c|c|}\hline
    x & f  & f' \\ \hline \hline
    1 & 2 & 4 \\ \hline
    2 & 1  & 0  \\ \hline
    3 & -2  & -2 \\ \hline
    4 & -3 & -3  \\ \hline
  \end{array}
  \]

Let $g(x) = (f^{-1})(2x)$.  Then the point $(1,\answer{1})$ is on the graph of $g$.

\[
g'(1) = \answer{\frac{1}{2}}
\]
